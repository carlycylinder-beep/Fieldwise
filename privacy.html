Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Fieldwise Group ("Fieldwise," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect when you visit thefieldwisegroup.com, how it's used, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

When you book a call through our scheduling tool (Calendly), you provide information such as your name, email address, phone number, and business details. We collect this directly from you, only when you choose to submit it — we do not collect this information passively.

How We Use Website Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this website — for example, which pages are viewed and how visitors found the site. Google Analytics uses cookies (small text files stored on your device) to collect this information. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. You can learn more about how Google handles this data at policies.google.com/privacy, and you can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

How We Use Your Information

Information you submit when booking a call is used solely to schedule and prepare for that conversation, and to follow up with you about Fieldwise's services. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use Calendly to manage scheduling. When you book a call, your information is also subject to Calendly's own privacy policy. We use Google Fonts to display text on this site, which may involve a request to Google's servers to load font files.

Your Choices

You may ask us at any time to tell you what information we have about you, or to delete it, by contacting us using the information below. You can also decline to provide information by simply not booking a call.

Confidentiality

Your email and contact information are never shared, sold, or used for unsolicited marketing. We respect your time and your inbox.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent version.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, reach out at hello@thefieldwisegroup.com or via LinkedIn.
